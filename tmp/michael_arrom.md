# Michael Arrom Music - Competitive Analysis

**Website:** https://michaelarrom.com/session-recording

## Overview
LA-based session musician with high-profile credits (Keith Urban, Steve Vai, Hunter Hayes). Professional presentation with strong credibility.

## Service Offering
- Remote session recording (piano, keys, synths)
- Multiple instruments available
- Professional studio quality
- Quick turnaround

## Major Credits
- Keith Urban
- Steve Vai
- Hunter Hayes
- USC professor credentials

## Strengths
- **Extremely professional presentation**
- Major artist credits build trust
- Clean, modern website design
- Multiple instrument options
- Clear service description

## Weaknesses
- **No pricing transparency** (must contact for quote)
- Limited educational content
- Minimal blog/SEO content
- No detailed process explanation
- Could benefit from more audio examples
- No clear turnaround time promises

## Key Takeaway
Professional presentation is crucial, but adding pricing transparency and detailed process workflow would improve conversion. Educational content missing.
