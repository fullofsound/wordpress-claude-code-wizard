# Vintage Keys Studio - Competitive Analysis

**Website:** https://vintagekeysstudio.com/blog/2015/4/17/bespoke-piano-recording

## Overview
UK-based bespoke piano recording service emphasizing perfect pitch ability and customized arrangements.

## Service Offering
- Custom piano recording tailored to client needs
- Perfect pitch ability (can learn songs by ear)
- Remote collaboration
- Multiple takes and performance options

## Strengths
- Unique "perfect pitch" selling point
- Personalized, bespoke approach
- Quality-focused messaging

## Weaknesses
- Blog-style presentation, not service-focused landing page
- **No clear pricing structure displayed**
- Limited visual portfolio/audio examples on main page
- Minimal SEO content
- No embedded audio player on service page
- Unclear process/workflow

## Key Takeaway
Need clear service pages with pricing, audio examples, and streamlined booking process - not just blog posts.
